Who am I?

My name is Areej Mazhar, a 20-year-old living in the country of Pakistan. I’m the middle child with two brothers. I come from a very humble family, taught to be kind and respectful to everyone, and to stand up for myself and others when given the chance. Compared to my family, I’m quite introverted and prefer to stay by my lonesome and stick to my own devices.

I like to think of myself as a creative person, occasionally sketching and painting. I have many of my own characters planned from their designs to their stories and interactions. Programming is one of my many passions, and I’m soon pursuing a bachelor’s degree in computer science. To prepare myself a bit more, I decided to take this course and have learned a lot!

Problem Set 8: Homepage

This is a portfolio type website for CS50 Introduction to Computer Science, problem set 8: homepage. I made a personal webpage with details about me, my hobbies and likes, as well as included a gallery all with HTML, CSS and a bit of JavaScript with some Bootstrap elements.
